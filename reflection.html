Reflection on Academics

In my time at Bradley, I have taken some awesome courses. Some of my favorites include:

  • CS 360 - Introduction to Data Science
  • CS 370 - Database Management Systems
  • CS 462 - Machine Learning
  • CS 463 - Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining

    •
    Clearly, in my studies, I have taken an interest in topics surrounding data science, and data in general. This is what lead me to pursuing an internship at Merck as a database programming intern in the summer of 2022. In this position, my academic experience and coursework definitely helped. It is nice to have learned so many technical skills in several different courses, but I think the criticial thinking skills I have picked up through projects has been the most valuable.
